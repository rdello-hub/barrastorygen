# Brand & Design Rules

## Visual Identity
The interface and generated stories must adhere to this exact, premium color palette:
- **Dark Navy**: `#101628` (Primary immersive background for canvas/app)
- **Royal Blue**: `#27509e` (Secondary accents, buttons, highlights)
- **Pale Yellow**: `#f5ff85` (Primary Call-to-Action, high-contrast text)

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: `Inter` (Google Fonts). Highly legible, clean sans-serif. No overly decorative fonts.

## Product Experience Tone
- **Interface**: Simple, immediate, premium, and clean. 
- **Not**: Overly technical, developer-looking, or overloaded.
- **Priority**: Speed of editing, visual clarity, and minimal friction.
- The user must feel that creating a story is a "click & go" process.
